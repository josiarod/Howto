How to say hello

If you travel to one of the destinations listed below you are going to be able to say at lest hello and cause a good first impression. Ready to learn

France

Formal: Bonjour
Informal: Salut

Spain

Formal: Hola
Informal: ¿Qué tal? (What’s up?)

Russia

Formal: Zdravstvuyte
Informal: Privet

Italy

Formal: Salve
Informal: Ciao

Germany

Formal: Guten Tag
Informal: Hallo, Hi
